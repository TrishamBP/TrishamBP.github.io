A little info about me!

Photo taken in Boston..I love the patriots and Brady is the GOAT!!

I grew up in Pune, India and now find myself across the Atlantic, where I graduated from Worcester Polytechnic Institute, with a Masters in Mechanical Engineering. I am currently working as a Teaching Assistant training senior year students on ANSYS-Structural and SOLIDWORKS. Prior to WPI I interned at Forbes Marshall India Pvt Ltd, primarily resolving design issues and modifying existing design related problems in steam traps, analyzing their performance in ANSYS-fluent, creating new Bill of Material and assist in manufacturing. I gained solid research, designing and problem-solving skills while working in one of the largest companies in India. During my studies at WPI, I was involved with projects pertaining to CFD modelling, analyzing Thermo-Fluid systems using SOLIDWORKS and ANSYS, prototype testing and drafting, these projects and experiences have provided me with a deeper understanding of multifaceted problems that companies encounter in their daily operations. I have completed several CFD modelling projects, in which I have modelled the air flow through an electronic box, catalytic converter and exhaust manifold. I have also developed several MATLAB codes to solve elliptical, hyperbolic and parabolic fluid dynamic equations.
